Amend the Comprehensive Protection Plan (“CPP”) as follows:
  
Effective January 1, 2007, renumber Sections 2.05 through 2.12 of CPP as Sections 2.06 through 2.13 of CPP, respectively.
Effective January 1, 2007, add new Section 2.05 of CPP as follows:
“Approved Conference-Controlled Extension Ministry List” shall mean a list of extension ministries reported annually to the Administrator by a Conference.  By reporting any such extension ministry, a Conference agrees to make contributions for Clergypersons under episcopal appointment by that Conference’s bishop to that extension ministry.  A Conference may add or remove extension ministries from the Approved Conference-Controlled Extension Ministry List annually as of a date specified from time to time by the Administrator during such reporting period as the Administrator may designate from time to time, but once an extension ministry is reported for the Approved Conference-Controlled Extension Ministry List, it will remain on the list until it is removed by a Conference as of a later annually specified date based on the Conference’s later report during a designated annual reporting period.

    Effective January 1, 2007, renumber Section 2.13 of CPP as Section 2.15 of CPP.
    Effective January 1, 2007, add new Section 2.14 of CPP as follows:
“Conference-Controlled Entity” shall mean any extension ministry (such as an agency, a camp, or a foundation) that is on a Conference’s Approved Conference-Controlled Ministry List.

Effective January 1, 2007, renumber Sections 2.14, 2.15 and 2.16 of CPP as Sections 2.16, 2.18 and 2.19 of CPP, respectively.
